Karahi

Mouth-watering chicken

Chicken Karahi is a popular dish across the globe and you must find it in Pakistani Restaurants. The name derives from the cooking pot “karahi” (wok) in which meat is cooked.

Ingredients

  • Chicken 1 kg
  • Yogurt 1 cup
  • Red chili powder 1 tsp
  • Salt 1½ tsp
  • Turmeric 1½ tsp
  • Cumin seeds 2 tsp (crushed)
  • Oil ½ cup
  • Coriander 1 tsp (crushed(
  • Green cardamom (4)
  • Cloves (4)
  • Onions 1 cup (chopped)
  • Ginger garlic paste 1 tbsp (chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 3 green chillies (chopped)
  • Garam masala powder 1 tsp
  • Coriandes leaves (handful)

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, chili powder, salt, turmeric, cumin, and coriander for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan; add green cardamom, cloves with onion. Fry till golden.
  3. Add in chopped ginger-garlic. Stir fry for 2 minutes
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add tomatoes and green chilies. Cover and cook till tomatoes soften and oil comes on top.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala powder; garnish with chopped coriander, green chilies, and ginger.
